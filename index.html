About Us

Welcome to The Royal Oak Steakhouse, a culinary cornerstone in the heart of Yorkshire since 1982. Our passion for premium beef and British hospitality has made us a beloved destination for steak enthusiasts across the United Kingdom. Founded by the Thompson family, our restaurant began as a humble pub serving locally-sourced meats. Over four decades, we've refined our craft, partnering with the finest cattle farms in the Yorkshire Dales and beyond to bring you an unparalleled dining experience.
